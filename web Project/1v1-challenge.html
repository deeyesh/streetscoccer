1v1 challenge

About

This 1v1 style of play aims to get players to be creative and strategic at scoring more goals than their opponents during a three minute match, in a limited amount of space Entry is free.



BASICS RULES
  • Each 1v1 match is three minutes long
  • Score goals inside the defending player’s half
  • No fouling and no slide tackling

Tournament Objectives

This style of play promotes creativity on the ball and fun competition. Each player plays four round robin matches against other players in his/her age bracket. After all round robin matches have been played, the top eight finishers in each age group play in quarterfinal matches. Winners then play for the Championship. All the rules of the regular match of soccer apply except slide tackling is not allowed and is treated as a foul (see rules below).

Matches
  • Ball Possession: Officials will use coin toss to determine who gets the ball first. The starting players start with the ball at their own end line. Also, when a goal is scored, the player who got scored on will start with the ball at his or her own end line. Ball will change possession after each goal.
  • Inbounding: The ball is started at the point of exit or anywhere behind that point of exit by dribbling in. The defensive player must give the player restarting play from out of bounds about a yard to amount of space.Once the ball touches the line it is in play and ‘live’.
  • Time Out: Only allowed by the official for an emergency.
  • Fouls: Any foul committed at any time results in a penalty kick.
  • Forfeiture(s): If a player arrives one minute late to the scheduled match - that is an automatic forfeiture. Any physical altercation started by a member in any given bracket will also result in automatic forfeiture, for that match (and possible for the tournament).

Playing Area

Area: 20ft. x 40ft. pitch.

Playing area has a center line.
